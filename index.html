Capabilities

Website design and development.

Building a website that serves the needs of both you and your customer isn't simply a matter of aesthetics. To create an effective website in both form and function we take every step from domain purchases to establishing professional email services for your team as seriously as design and deployment.

Impactful search engine optimization.

Ensuring your site reaches the top of search queries related to your business or product is essential for boosting visitors to your site and increasing sales. Using Google’s Webmaster tools and a host of SEO best practices, I’ll help you navigate the complex waters of SEO management to ensure your customers can find the products and services that you provide.

Seamless upgrades and customization.

Can’t find a plan to fit your needs or need add that essential feature? Contact me to discuss a custom plan with flexible feature based pricing or simply purchase additional features even after your project has been initiated or completed.

Responsive, mobile optimized design.

Responsive design is a technique utilized to develop sites that provide an optimal viewing and navigation experience across a wide range of devices from desktop computers to mobile phones. With over 76% of Canadians browsing the web with their mobile phones, responsive design is essential to adapting your content to this growing platform. Your website will employ mobile friendly navigation and design to ensure a seamless experience across all user devices.

Content updates and maintenence.

Need to update your site or encounter a problem? All plans come with up to 3 months of free content updates and technical support past the official deployment of your custom site. If you plan to make frequent changes or content updates to your site consider purchasing additional support when completing the project planner or utilize the robust content management system included with each site.
