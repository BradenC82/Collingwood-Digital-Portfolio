Project Planner.

Frequently asked questions.

"If I already own a domain can it be transfered over to my new site?"

Any domain you currently own may be transfered to your new site hassle free. Note that if you choose to reuse your previous domain, your previous site will be replaced by your new site.

"Do you accept international clients?"

Although clients within the city of Winnipeg have the option of meeting with me personally to discuss their projects, out of province or international clients can receive updates and discuss project through email, phone or video chat.

"Will monthly hosting costs be covered by my purchase plan?"

Unfortunately your monthly hosting costs are not covered by your website purchase plan. Instead hosting is handled through a third party company that will vary depending on the needs of your project and will be an additional cost outside of your purchase plan.

"Do you develop multilingual sites?"

Both Professional and Premium pricing plans include support for 1 additional language. Support for beyond 2 languages will result in additional cost, calculated on a case by case basis. All translations must be provided.

"Will refunds be given after the termination of a project due to unforseen circumstances?"

"In order to protect the labour invested into a project all payments must be made upfront before a project moves forward.Refunds cannot be returned once work on a project begins."
